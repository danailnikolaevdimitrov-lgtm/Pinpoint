Pinpoint

A secret location is hidden somewhere in Bulgaria. No hints — just the border and your instincts. Navigate blind, guess by guess, and close in before time runs out.

How to Play
🎯

The Target

A secret location is hidden somewhere in Bulgaria. Your mission is to find it.

📍

Place Your Pin

Click anywhere inside the Bulgarian border. Clicks outside are ignored.

🔒

Lock In

Press Lock In Guess to confirm. You will see how many km off you were — use it to close in.

⭐

Victory

Land within the winning radius to win. Rounds are limited — every guess counts.
